House washing

Palencia house washing for stucco, trim, soffits, and exterior buildup.

Palencia homes collect algae, mildew, spider webs, and irrigation stains because of humidity, shade, landscaping, and overspray. House washing should be framed as soft washing and exterior cleaning, not blasting the home with pressure.

Soft wash

House washing belongs under pressure washing, but the copy should say soft wash.

The page should avoid making it sound like high pressure is used on delicate exterior finishes. Sell safe cleaning, curb appeal, and maintenance.

  1. Inspect: check plants, fixtures, oxidation, and problem areas.
  2. Treat: apply the right cleaning approach for organics.
  3. Rinse: remove buildup from the exterior surface.
  4. Review: walk the property and check touch-up areas.
